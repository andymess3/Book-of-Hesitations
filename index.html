Unicorns.

Is this REALLY in the Bible? or

"Money is the root of all evil."

Is this REALLY in the Bible? or

Not in the Bible. In the New Testament, Paul says to Timothy, "The LOVE of money is the root of all evil." So while the difference between this common phrase and what is actually in the Bible is subtle, they are different none the less.
